Will Brickner | Art
These videos are a mix of visualizations of the behavior of iterative complex functions, chaotic systems (n-body simulations), recursion, and simple parametric functions.

All of these videos were made using Videra, a specialized rendering engine I created.

Many of these videos contain modified audio from Soundcloud. Below each video you can find a link to the original (unaltered) audio.

More can be found on my instagram: @computer.graphics
